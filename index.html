I prioritized and addressed bugs, ensuring timely resolutions and contributing to the overall quality assurance process. My proactive approach facilitated better communication between QA and development teams.
  • Agile Methodologies: Actively participated in implementing agile methodologies throughout the software development lifecycle. I engaged in sprint planning and retrospectives, ensuring that testing strategies aligned with project goals and timelines.

My role as a QA Automation Engineer in the TimeClock Plus Software project was instrumental in delivering a reliable and efficient product.
